Detailed Description

row iterators are designed to iterate on rows of the data file.

Examples:

  • Iterator on the data file:@code rowIterator('obsIter')
  • Iterator on another iterator:
    1 metaIterator('personIter','__dataFile__','panelObsIter','Id')
    2 rowIterator('panelObsIter','personIter')
